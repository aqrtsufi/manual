Shaykh Nishaat Ahmed Siddiqi 

The key is to look with the eye of the heart. 

Where love is established, unity becomes it natural manifestation. 

It is the hands that reveal the reality of what is in your heart. That is why service is so important. It is the key. 

When you live in remembrance, there is an unveiling. You live with Allah.

Life is beautiful when it is simple. 

Follow the path but let love light the way.

The Sufi path is the art of preparing the heart to receive.

Our goal is not to perfect ourselves, but to perfect our connection to Allah. In that connection, true peace is found. 

Perhaps the greatest worship is to fully be what you were meant to be.

Your Shaykh is a mirror. When you look at him with Love you will see yourself with Love.

We want to be perfect. But we lose sincerity. But if I am sincere, Allah will show me how perfect He is. 

Every struggle where does it come from? Allah. So where does it lead to? Allah.

When you surrender you trust. When you trust you surrender.

Humility is not thinking less of yourself, it is thinking of yourself less. 

The Sufi learns to look beyond the gift to the Giver. Every state, whether ease or hardship, carries a provision. If it brings us closer to Allah, then it is among the greatest forms of rizq.

Sufism is not about running away from the world, or for that matter, chasing it. It is about being present with Allah in every moment. In responding to what He wants from us right now, with sincerity and presence. 

It is not magic, it is Al Rahman responding to surrender.
